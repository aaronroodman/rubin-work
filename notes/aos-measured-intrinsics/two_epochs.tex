\title{The ``two families'' in the FAM measured intrinsic are two observing epochs}
\author{Aaron Roodman}
\setDocRef{XXXTN-NNN}                                        % <-- your series handle
\date{\today}

\setDocAbstract{%
The two distinct low-order intrinsic patterns seen when the $\pm30/\pm45$ rotator
bins are added to the AOS FAM measured-intrinsic build are not a build artifact:
they are two different observing epochs (March vs April~9) with a different M1M3
axial thermal gradient, and the rotator binning coincides exactly with the epoch
split.  Averaging the two epochs into one measured intrinsic is what inflates the
residuals.}
